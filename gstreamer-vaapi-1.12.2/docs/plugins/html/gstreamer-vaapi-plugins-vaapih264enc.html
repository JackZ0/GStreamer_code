Description

Encodes raw video streams into H.264 bitstreams.

Example launch line

1
 
gst-launch-1.0 -ev videotestsrc num-buffers=60 ! timeoverlay ! vaapih264enc ! h264parse ! mp4mux ! filesink location=test.mp4
